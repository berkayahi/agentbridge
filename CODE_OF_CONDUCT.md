# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless
of background, identity, experience, appearance, disability, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while taking responsibility for impact.
- Accept technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Protect private deployment and security information.
- Help keep discussions focused on the project.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, sustained
disruption, and publishing another person's private information are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary. Enforcement decisions
should consider context, impact, prior behavior, and community safety.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's
private security reporting channel. Maintainers will limit disclosure, avoid
conflicts of interest, and respond as promptly as practical.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the
project.
